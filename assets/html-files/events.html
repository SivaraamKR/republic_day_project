Vande Bharatam Nritya Utsav 2023

   JAN 21 - 29 2023

Vande Bharatam - Nritya Utsav, an all-India dance competition is an initiative taken by the Ministry of Culture as part of Republic Day Celebrations 2023 and under the aegis of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav to work towards promoting the spirit of 'Jan Bhagidari', while also showcasing India's vibrancy thorough its dance. The competition will be held at the state and zonal level, and will culminate with the grand finale on 18th December, 2022 in New Delhi.

Rewards - The Winners will perform at Republic Day Celebrations 2023



Veer Gatha Project 2023

   DEC 21- JAN 20 2023

To honour the acts of bravery and sacrifice of the officers / personnel of the Armed Forces, other lawfully constituted forces and civilians, gallantry awards are announced twice in a year – first on the occasion of the Republic Day and then on the Occasion of the Independence Day. In order to disseminate the details of bravery acts and the life stories of these brave-hearts among the students, it has been proposed by the Ministry of Defence that school students may be motivated to do projects/activities based on gallantry award winners.

Rewards - Each of the 25 winners will be invited for the Republic Day Celebration at Delhi.
